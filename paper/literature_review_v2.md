# Literature Review v2 — Key Competitors and Positioning (March 2026)

## Must-Cite Papers (Priority)

1. **MADT (arXiv 2602.02903, 2026)** — DT + multi-agent TSC. Our closest competitor. Differentiate: we handle EV preemption.
2. **OffLight (arXiv 2411.06601, 2024)** — Offline MARL for TSC. Key offline RL competitor. Differentiate: DT vs value-based, EV capability.
3. **EMVLight (TRC 2023)** — MARL for EV routing + preemption. Online RL. Differentiate: we're offline.
4. **X-Light (IJCAI 2024)** — Transformer for TSC at top venue. Cross-city transfer.
5. **LLMLight (KDD 2025)** — LLM for TSC. Differentiate: efficiency.
6. **DT-Powered Traffic Control (IEEE 2024)** — Direct DT-for-TSC precursor. Single intersection.
7. **HarmoDT (ICML 2024)** — Multi-task DT.
8. **UNREST (CoRL 2024 Oral)** — Uncertainty-aware DT.
9. **Traffic-R1 (2025, deployed)** — LLM for TSC in production.

## Key Gap Our Paper Fills
No existing work combines Decision Transformers with emergency vehicle preemption.
Offline learning for EV preemption is completely unexplored.
